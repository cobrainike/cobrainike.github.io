SubWatch Payment Redirect Pages

This directory contains the payment redirect pages for the SubWatch Chrome Extension.

Environment: staging
Last Updated: 2026-01-20 23:45:07

Available Pages

  • Success Page - Payment successful redirect
  • Failure Page - Payment failed redirect

Usage

These pages are used as redirect URLs for Viva Wallet payment processing:

  • Success URL: https://cobrainike.github.io/subwatch/success.html
  • Failure URL: https://cobrainike.github.io/subwatch/failure.html

About SubWatch

SubWatch is an AI-powered subscription tracker that helps you manage your recurring payments and avoid unwanted charges.
